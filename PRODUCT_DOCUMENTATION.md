## ü§ñ Product 1: ANU 6.0 Robot Hardware

### Specifications

#### Physical Dimensions
- **Height**: 45 cm
- **Weight**: 2.5 kg
- **DOF**: 17 degrees of freedom
- **Material**: 3D printed PLA/ABS + metal servos

#### Hardware Components
- **Main Controller**: Raspberry Pi 5 (8GB RAM)
- **Servo Motors**: 17x MG995 servos
- **Servo Driver**: PCA9685 (16-channel PWM)
- **Camera**: HD USB Webcam (720p)
- **Microphone**: USB directional microphone
- **Speaker**: Bluetooth speaker (5W)
- **Sensors**: 
  - Ultrasonic sensor (HC-SR04)
  - 2x PIR motion sensors
  - Temperature sensor (DHT11)
- **Power**: 5V 5A power supply + battery backup
